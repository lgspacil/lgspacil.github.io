About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer that graduated Coding Dojo in 2017, a rigorous full-stack software development training bootcamp. I have been working as a web development intern creating an auction website for West Valley Medical.

I graduated from the University of California, Davis with a bachelor's degree in Environmental Science. I then became interested in programming while working on Google/Waymo's self driving car project. There I discovered the power coding holds and the endless opportunities it can create. I decided to make the transition into web development by teaching myself for 2 years and then joining Coding Dojo.

Aside from coding, I have a passion for travel and discovering new places. My two most recent adventures have taken me to Thailand and Iceland.
